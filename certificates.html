Quality management system

AS9100D & ISO9001:2015

LETAR’s Quality Management System is ISO 9001:2015 and AS9100 Rev. D certified.

A manufacturing plan is prepared for each order, including inspection procedures, to ensure conformance to customer specifications and requirements. Quality control personnel conduct and record in-process and final inspections as directed by the plan. All incoming material and documentation is examined by Receiving Inspection, marked for identification and stored in a controlled area. Certification records are maintained in controlled filing systems. The procedures have been documented in detail in LETAR’s Quality Manual, and can be provided upon request.
