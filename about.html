About Letar

Two decades of parts
that arrive with their paperwork.

Letar Inc. is a precision CNC machine shop in Concord, Ontario, serving the Aerospace & Defence industry for over twenty years — with long-term relationships with OEMs and Tier 1 companies, including approval as a Bombardier supplier.

Who we are

Built for flight-critical work

Aerospace is where we grew up. The disciplines it demands — controlled processes, certified material, full traceability from mill cert to shipped serial number — run through everything we machine, whether the part flies or not.

Our floor runs 3-, 4-, and 5-axis CNC milling and turning alongside conventional machining, fabrication, coordinated chemical conversion and finishing, and assembly. Concurrent engineering is part of the quote: we review manufacturability before the first chip, and commit to lead times we can keep.

The same team that quotes your part programs it, machines it, inspects it, and ships it with its full documentation package. That's why customers who came for one part stay for a decade.
