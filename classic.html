How a part moves through Letar

One traveler, four disciplines

01 · Program

Concurrent engineering

We join the job at the drawing stage — free consultations on new concepts and revisions, manufacturability feedback before the first chip.

02 · Machine

3, 4 & 5-axis machining

Milling and turning across 26 CNC machines — envelopes to 118″ × 32″ × 28″ in aluminum, titanium, steels, copper, brass, and plastics.

03 · Inspect

In-house CMM verification

Quality checked throughout production on in-house CMM technology — AS9102 first articles, certs of conformance, full material traceability.

04 · Assemble & ship

Sub-assembly & delivery

Nutplates, inserts, press fits, bonding, and conversion coating in-house — parts arrive assembled, documented, and ready to install.
