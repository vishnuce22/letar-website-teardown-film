Our capabilities

Assembly

Our commitment to precision extends beyond machining and into comprehensive assembly solutions. We offer hardware installation services, including the integration of bushings, bearings, inserts, and pins, ensuring each component is securely fitted to exact specifications.

Primary Assembly Capabilities

  • Nutplates & Rivets
  • Press Fit Assemblies
  • Bolting & Screwing
  • Helicoils
  • Threaded Inserts

Complementary Capabilities

  • Electrical Bonding
  • Testing of Physical Properties
  • Hardness & Electrical Conductivity Testing
  • Chemical Conversion Treatment
  • Application of Fluid Resistant Primer
  • Reaming & Final Fit

Our team adheres to strict quality standards and customer specification requirements, guaranteeing that every sub-assembly is built reliably. By providing in-house assembly, we streamline the supply chain, reduce lead times, and deliver fully integrated solutions that meet the highest industry standards.
