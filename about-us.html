Letar Inc · Concord, Ontario

Precision, proven for two decades.

For over 25 years, OEM’s and Tier-1 companies across North America have trusted Letar for precision-machined parts and assemblies — from aerospace and defence to telecommunication and medical robotics. Every shipment carries the same standard: reliable delivery, exceptional service, and the highest quality components.

Standards driven

Certified, registered, approved

Letar Inc. is certified to AS9100D & ISO9001:2015, registered to the Controlled Goods Program (CGP), US Joint Certification Program (JCP), and on several ASL’s — including Bombardier.

Our capabilities include 3, 4, & 5-axis machining — milling and lathe, fabrication, chemical conversion, and assembly across a vast array of material types. We provide sub-assembly work, including bushings, bearings, inserts, panels, and spoilers. Letar Inc. offers concurrent engineering services to assist with significant revisions or the manufacturing and design of new part concepts.
