Quality & compliance

Audited before
your auditor arrives.

Every credential below is maintained through recurring third-party audits — current certificates are available to customers on request.

AS9100D

The aerospace quality management standard — certified since 2021 and maintained through recurring surveillance audits. Contract review, configuration control, in-process verification, and delivery — audited end to end, with records retained for the life your customers require.

ISO 9001

The foundation quality system under our AS9100D certification — process discipline, corrective action, and continual improvement across the whole business.

ITAR registered

Registered to machine U.S. defence articles, with CCR registration and certification under the U.S./Canada Joint Certification Program (JCP). Technical data is access-controlled to authorized persons only, on the floor and in our systems.

Controlled Goods Program

Approved under Canada's CGP by PWGSC and recognized by the U.S. Department of Defense — security-assessed personnel and controlled handling of examination-listed goods and technical data.
