Every certification we maintain represents a promise to our customers — that your parts are made right, documented completely, and traceable forever.

AS9100D Rev. D — Aerospace Quality Management System

IAQG · Current certification · Re-certified on a 3-year cycle

AS9100D is the highest-level quality management standard for the aerospace and defence manufacturing industry, published by the International Aerospace Quality Group (IAQG) and based on ISO 9001:2015 with additional aerospace-specific requirements. Our certification covers the complete scope of our operations — from order entry and material receiving through machining, inspection, and final delivery.

Every work order has a Manufacturing Plan with documented operation sequence, tooling, and inspection steps
In-process inspection at every critical feature — not just final inspection
First Article Inspection Reports (FAIR) per AS9102 available on request
Nonconformance (NCR) management with mandatory disposition and root cause — closed loop
Annual surveillance audits by accredited registrar; full re-certification every 3 years
What it means for you: complete traceability from raw material cert to final inspection record, retained for 10+ years

ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management System

International Organization for Standardization · Current

ISO 9001:2015 is the internationally recognized baseline quality management standard, and the foundation upon which AS9100D is built. Our ISO certification is embedded within our AS9100D registration — any supplier approved under AS9100D automatically meets ISO 9001. For customers outside the aerospace sector, ISO 9001 provides the same systematic quality assurance framework.

Systematic process control and continual improvement methodology
Risk-based thinking applied to all production operations
Customer satisfaction measurement and feedback integration
Supplier qualification and management process

ITAR Registered

U.S. International Traffic in Arms Regulations · DDTC

Letar Inc. is registered and compliant with the U.S. International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR), administered by the Directorate of Defense Trade Controls. Combined with our Canadian CGP and JCP approvals, this lets U.S. and Canadian primes share defence-related technical data and hardware with us inside a fully compliant cross-border framework.

Compliant handling of ITAR-controlled technical data and hardware
Segregated, access-controlled environment for controlled work
Personnel security controls for staff handling controlled items
Works hand-in-hand with CGP + JCP for seamless US/Canada programs

CGP — Controlled Goods Program

Canada · Department of Public Works and Government Services · Defence Production Act

The Controlled Goods Program (CGP) is Canada's domestic regime for protecting controlled defense goods and technology under the Defence Production Act. CGP approval authorizes Letar Inc. to examine, possess, and transfer controlled goods — a prerequisite for work on Canadian defense programs and for receiving export-controlled goods from U.S. ITAR registrants.

Registered under the Controlled Goods Regulations
Security assessment of all personnel with access to controlled goods
Designated Official (DO) responsible for CGP compliance
Complements ITAR for seamless cross-border US/Canada defense programs
JCP

JCP — Joint Certification Program

U.S. / Canada Bilateral Agreement · DDA & PWGSC

The Joint Certification Program is a bilateral agreement between the U.S. and Canadian governments enabling certified companies to share unclassified military technical data across the border without individual export licenses. JCP certification eliminates redundant export-license paperwork for most US/Canada aerospace and defence programs.

Enables receipt of ITAR-controlled technical data from U.S. primes without individual export licenses
Simplifies the supply chain for North American cross-border programs
Reduces lead time on program startup — no waiting for ITAR export license approvals
