Standards & Compliance

Built to the Highest Standards

Every certification we hold is a promise to our customers — that every part we ship meets the most demanding requirements in the industry.

AS9100D Rev. D
IAQG Aerospace QMS · Current Certification

The gold standard for aerospace quality management. Every process, from order entry to delivery, is documented, controlled, and audited — giving customers complete traceability on every part.

ISO 9001:2015
Quality Management System

Internationally recognized quality management framework underpinning our AS9100D certification. Systematic control of all processes, continual improvement, and customer-focused delivery.

CGP — Controlled Goods
Canada Defence Production Act

Approved under Canada's Controlled Goods Program — authorization to examine, possess, and transfer controlled defence goods and technology within Canada.

ITAR Registered
U.S. International Traffic in Arms Regulations

Registered and compliant for the handling of defence-related technical data and hardware — enabling secure work on controlled U.S. and cross-border defence programs.

JCP
JCP Certified

Bilateral U.S.–Canada certification enabling cross-border aerospace manufacturing without redundant export restrictions — seamless supply chain integration for North American prime contractors.
