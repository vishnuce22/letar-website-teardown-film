What We Machine

5-Axis

5-Axis Simultaneous Milling

Matsuura MAM72 · DMG DMU 60 EVO · DMG DMU 95 · Mazak Variaxis i700

Our 5-axis fleet handles the most geometrically complex aerospace parts — impellers, structural brackets, turbine housings, and actuator bodies — with simultaneous 5-axis contouring that minimizes setups and maximizes accuracy.

Simultaneous A/B/C-axis contouring
Single-setup complex geometry — fewer datums, tighter GD&T
Undercut features, deep cavities, compound angles
Tolerance capability: ±0.0005" on critical features
4-Axis

4-Axis CNC Milling

Mazak MX-420 · MX-330 · Multiple VMCs

Continuous rotary 4th-axis machining for parts requiring access to multiple faces without re-fixturing. Ideal for production volumes where cycle time matters.

Full 360° continuous rotation
Indexing for multi-face operations in single setup
Excellent for flanges, cylinder bodies, eccentric parts
Standard tolerance: ±0.001"
3-Axis

3-Axis CNC Milling

Multiple Mazak VMCs · Haas VF Series

High-throughput 3-axis milling for prismatic parts and high-volume production. Our fleet of VMCs runs around the clock on repetitive precision work.

High-speed machining (HSM) up to 15,000 RPM
Large work envelopes up to 1000 × 560 × 660mm
Production runs from 1 to 10,000+ pieces
Standard tolerance: ±0.001"

Materials

Materials We Machine

We work in the full range of aerospace-grade metals and engineering plastics — with documented material traceability on every job.

Aluminum Alloys
6061-T6 · 7075-T6 · 2024-T3 · 5052 · 6063
Structural housings, brackets, panels, antenna structures, avionics enclosures. Best strength-to-weight ratio for non-critical fatigue parts.
Titanium
Ti-6Al-4V (Grade 5) · Grade 2 CP
Flight-critical structural components, implants, and high-temperature applications. Our 5-axis centres are optimized for titanium's work-hardening characteristics.
Stainless Steel
303 · 304 · 316L · 17-4 PH · 15-5 PH · 410
Hydraulic fittings, actuator components, medical parts, and food/pharmaceutical equipment requiring corrosion resistance and high strength.
Steel Alloys
4140 · 4340 · 1018 · 1045 · H13 · D2
Gears, shafts, structural components, and tooling requiring hardness and wear resistance. Heat-treat coordinated with certified suppliers.
Brass & Copper
C360 Free-Machining Brass · C110 Copper
Electrical connectors, waveguide components, bushings, RF shielding, and thermal management parts for telecom and defence applications.
Engineering Plastics
PEEK · Delrin (Acetal) · Nylon · PTFE · Ultem
Lightweight bushings, insulators, EMI spacers, and non-metallic components for aerospace and medical assemblies where metal is not suitable.
