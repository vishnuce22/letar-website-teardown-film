Telecommunication

Supporting North American Emergency Communications for Over 20 Years

Letar Inc. is proud to support the production of a rugged wireless communication device used by First Responders across North America. For over fifteen years, we have been a trusted machining partner in the telecommunications space, manufacturing matched sets of precision parts that meet stringent waterproofing, impact resistance, and critical tolerance requirements.

Scope of Capabilities Includes

  • Enclosures
  • Chassis
  • Filters
  • Heatsinks
  • Covers
  • Partitions

Telecommunication Components

  • Custom heatsinks
  • Cellular repeater casings
  • Precision housings for a range of end-uses

In addition to First Responder technologies, Letar Inc. also manufactures a variety of telecommunication components. All components are produced to exacting standards, supporting the evolving needs of telecommunications OEMs across North America.
